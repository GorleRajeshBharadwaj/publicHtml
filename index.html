About this item

Product details

Use the KitchenAid Asteroid Pot Holders 2 Pack to protect hands from the heat when handling hot pots, pans, and dishes. These oval pot holders each measure 6.5 inches wide by 10 inches long and feature a slide pocket for your hand for easy on and off use while you're cooking and baking. The heavy-duty, 100 percent cotton fabric offers comfort, flexibility, and insulation. Textured, silicone print grips on the pot holders are heat resistant and also ensure a more firm and secure grip when handling hot cookware. The matching pot holders feature a convenient hanging loop to easily display or store them when not in use. They are machine washable for easy care. Use your KitchenAid Asteroid Pot Holders 2 Pack along with coordinating kitchen accessories from KitchenAid including oven mitts and kitchen towels for a complete set of KitchenAid kitchen essentials! Each item is sold separately. Made in China.
  • HEAT RESISTANT COTTON – Made of 100 percent durable, heat-resistant cotton, these oven mitts will help shield your hands from hot pots and pans.
  • SILICONE PRINT GRIPS - Textured, heart-shaped silicone grips ensure a more secure hold when handling hot cookware.
  • SET OF TWO – Protect both hands while handling hot pots and pans. Set includes two (2) KitchenAid Asteroid Pot Holders in honey orange, 6.5" W x 10" L.
  • HANGING LOOP - Features a hanging loop for displaying or storing pot holders when not in use.
  • EASY CARE - Machine washable for easy care.
